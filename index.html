Who We Are

Founded by JDR (JakeDRavioli) in 2025, vecShift Studios is the place for future visions in indie development.

Originally coming off of Roblox as Dev4Fun Studios, vecShift has evolved its efforts to focus primarily on high-fidelity Unreal Engine 5 projects and accessible HTML5 web games.

We don't just want to make games; we strive to make the entire Indie Games ecosphere absolutely awesome.
